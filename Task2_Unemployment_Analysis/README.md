## 💡 Key Insights

1. Unemployment **spiked from ~8% to 23%+** during COVID-19 lockdown (Apr–May 2020)
2. **Urban areas** were hit harder than rural areas
3. **North & East India** had consistently higher unemployment
4. **South & West India** (Gujarat, Goa, Tamil Nadu) had the lowest rates
5. States with **low labour participation** often mask hidden unemployment
